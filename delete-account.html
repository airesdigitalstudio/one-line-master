Account and data deletion policy

Delete my account

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, for free, directly within the app or by email. See below how to do it and what happens to each type of data.

How to delete your account in the app
1
Open One Line Master on your device.
2
Go to your Profile or open the Settings menu.
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete my account".
4
Confirm the action. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

What happens to your data

When you delete your account, your data is handled as follows:

Data typeWhat happensTimeframe
Profile (username, avatar, country)DeletedImmediately
Email linked to your accountDeletedWithin 30 days
Wallet (coins, gems, Elite Pass, boosters)DeletedImmediately
Personal level recordsDeletedImmediately
Friends list and challengesDeletedImmediately
Purchased avatars and skinsDeletedImmediately
Public global records (leaderboard)AnonymizedWithin 30 days
Hall of Fame (historical records)AnonymizedKept without identification
⚠️ Warning: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your points, achievements, Elite Pass and virtual currencies will be permanently lost. In-app purchases are non-refundable after account deletion.

Prefer to request by email?

If you cannot access the app, send an email to [Suporte.ai](https://Suporte.ai)resdigital@[gmail.com](https://gmail.com) with the subject "Account Deletion – One Line Master" and include your username. We will process your request within 5 business days.
