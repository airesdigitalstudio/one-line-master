Fair Play Rules

Last updated: May 2026

One Line Master is a competitive game based on skill, logic, and fair competition. These Fair Play Rules exist to ensure a fair experience for all players.

Allowed

  • Playing manually using your own skills.
  • Competing in rankings and seasonal events.
  • Using official game features.

Prohibited Activities

  • Using bots or automation software.
  • Using auto-clickers or macros.
  • Using modified APK files.
  • Using hacked versions of the game.
  • Manipulating scores or rankings.
  • Exploiting bugs or glitches.
  • Creating multiple accounts to gain unfair advantages.
  • Farming advertisements fraudulently.
  • Using emulators or tools specifically modified to cheat.
  • Interfering with game servers, APIs, or security systems.
  • Attempting to bypass game restrictions.

Artificial Intelligence

Players may not use external software, scripts, automated systems, or artificial intelligence tools to automatically solve puzzles, complete levels, or gain ranking advantages.

Leaderboard Integrity

Global rankings, country rankings, seasonal rankings, and Hall of Fame placements are monitored for suspicious activity.

Investigation Process

The developer reserves the right to review accounts, scores, gameplay records, and other relevant data when suspicious activity is detected.

Penalties

  • Score removal.
  • Leaderboard removal.
  • Season reward cancellation.
  • Temporary suspension.
  • Permanent account ban.

Season Rewards and Prizes

If prizes, rewards, badges, profile frames, gift cards, electronics, or other rewards are offered, eligibility may require verification to ensure compliance with these Fair Play Rules.

Appeals

Players may contact Aires Digital Studio to request a review of enforcement actions.

Changes

These Fair Play Rules may be updated at any time to improve game security and fairness.
