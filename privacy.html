Privacy Policy — One Line Master

Last updated: June 6, 2026
Effective date: June 6, 2026

1. Introduction

One Line Master ("we", "our", or "the game") is a puzzle game developed and operated by its creator ("Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights as a user.

By downloading, installing, or playing One Line Master, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Identity Data

When you create a profile in One Line Master, we collect:

  • Username — chosen by you, displayed publicly on rankings.
  • Email address — used for account authentication, friend invitations, and challenge notifications.
  • Avatar seed — a code that generates your avatar image. Not a real photo.
  • Country — optionally associated with your profile and used for country-based rankings.

2.2 Game Performance Data

  • Total points accumulated across all levels.
  • Levels completed.
  • World records.
  • Level records.
  • Global records.
  • Season records.
  • ExRecord historical records.

2.3 Social & Interaction Data

  • Friendship data.
  • Challenge data.
  • Challenge results.
  • Friend requests status.

2.4 Economy & Virtual Goods Data

  • Coins and Gems.
  • Elite Pass status.
  • Elite Pass XP.
  • Daily ads watched.
  • Daily reward streak.
  • Last claimed reward date.
  • Active boosters.
  • Purchased avatars.
  • Unlocked skins.
  • Active skin.

2.5 Custom Level Data

  • Level name.
  • Pack.
  • Difficulty.
  • Dots and edges.
  • Public/private visibility status.

2.6 Technical & Device Data

  • IP address.
  • Device type and operating system.
  • Session timestamps.
  • User agent and browser information.

We do not directly collect device advertising IDs (GAID/IDFA), but third-party advertising SDKs may do so.

4. How Data Is Stored

All game data is stored on Base44 cloud infrastructure, running on enterprise-grade cloud providers.

  • Data may be stored in Brazil and/or the United States.
  • Data is retained while your account remains active.
  • Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
  • Anonymous and aggregate statistics may be retained.
